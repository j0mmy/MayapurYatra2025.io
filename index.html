About Mayapur

The Spiritual Heart of Bengal

Mayapur, located at the confluence of the sacred Ganges and Jalangi rivers, is the birthplace of Sri Chaitanya Mahaprabhu and the worldwide headquarters of ISKCON (International Society for Krishna Consciousness).

Our carefully curated yatra offers a deep spiritual experience, allowing you to connect with the divine energy of this sacred land while learning about the rich Vaishnava traditions under the guidance of experienced devotees.

Home to the magnificent Temple of the Vedic Planetarium and numerous historical sites, Mayapur offers visitors a unique blend of spiritual enlightenment, cultural immersion, and peaceful reflection.

Yatra Itinerary

Day 1: Arrival & Welcome

Arrival in Kolkata and transfer to Mayapur. Welcome ceremony at the ISKCON campus followed by evening aarti and introductory discourse.

Day 2: Temple of Vedic Planetarium

Morning program with mangala aarti, japa meditation, and breakfast prasadam. Full guided tour of the TOVP, followed by spiritual discourses and evening kirtan.

Day 3: Nabadwip Parikrama

Full-day parikrama (pilgrimage walk) to significant sites in Nabadwip associated with Lord Chaitanya's pastimes, with storytelling at each location.

Day 4: Yoga & Knowledge

Morning yoga session, followed by workshops on Bhagavad Gita and Vedic living. Afternoon visit to Srila Prabhupada's Samadhi and bhajan kutir.

Day 5: Ganga Experience

Morning boat ride on the sacred Ganges, visit to Yogapith (birthplace of Lord Chaitanya), and evening Ganga aarti ceremony.

Day 6: Cultural Immersion

Traditional Bengali cultural program, cooking class on preparing prasadam, and special darshan of Sri Sri Radha Madhava.

Day 7: Departure

Final morning program, closing ceremony with blessings, and departure to Kolkata with packed prasadam.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the yatra package? +

Our comprehensive package includes airport transfers from Kolkata, accommodation in Mayapur, all meals (prasadam), guided tours of all sites, spiritual programs, workshops, special darshans, and a devotional gift pack. International airfare to Kolkata is not included.

Do I need to have prior knowledge of Krishna consciousness? +

No prior knowledge is required. Our yatra is designed to be accessible and meaningful for everyone from beginners to advanced practitioners. Our experienced guides will provide context and explanations throughout the journey.

What should I pack for the yatra? +

We recommend bringing modest clothing that covers shoulders and knees, comfortable walking shoes, personal toiletries, any required medications, a water bottle, notebook and pen, and optional items like a prayer shawl, japa mala beads, and a camera. A detailed packing list will be provided upon registration.

Is there a dress code for temple visits? +

Yes, for temple visits, men should wear dhoti or pants (no shorts), and women should wear sarees, long skirts, or salwar kameez. We encourage traditional Vaishnava dress but it's not mandatory. Western clothing is acceptable as long as it's modest and respectful.

Will there be internet connectivity? +

Yes, there is Wi-Fi available in the accommodation areas, though we encourage participants to minimize digital distractions to fully immerse in the spiritual experience. Mobile network coverage is also available throughout Mayapur.

Can you accommodate special dietary requirements? +

All meals served are vegetarian prasadam. We can accommodate vegan, gluten-free, and most allergy-related dietary requirements with advance notice. Please mention your specific needs in your registration form.
